cookies

A cookie (American English), or a biscuit (British English), is a baked or cooked snack or dessert that is typically small, flat and sweet. It usually contains flour, sugar, egg, and some type of oil, fat, or butter. It may include other ingredients such as raisins, oats, chocolate chips, nuts, etc. Unlike other desserts, cookies do not require additional utensils or tableware. Simply pick up and enjoy. Whether you're tossing a couple in with your lunch or feeding an entire congregation, cookies provide a convenient and favorable treat for any occasion.
